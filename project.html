WELCOME TO BINSTARTER

BinStarter is a decentralized mining-style approval mechanism insurance protocol that is designed to examine projects according to certain criteria for 30 days and to protect liquidity providers against possible financial damages or any negativities.

Read more

BinStarter services include:

  • IDO Launch
  • Staking and Rewards
  • Whitelist Allocation
  • Allocation Rights of NFT Pools
  • Yield Farming
  • Insured Fixed Swap Pools


BINSTARTER STAKING POOLS

How Do Staking Pools Work?

Stakers need to meet the minimum required $BSR to enter the pool for IDO allocation. For example: a user with 3000 $BSR can stake in pool 2 as it meets pool 2 requirements.
Also note that the unlock period starts when a staker unstakes their $BSR holdings from the pools.
